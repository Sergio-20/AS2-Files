American Self Storage Corporate

American Self-Storage is an Oklahoma owned company serving Oklahoma and Wichita, KS with 23 facilities, and over 30 years of experience. The Company offers a large selection of affordable, secure storage for your personal storage, boat and RV storage and business storage solutions. At American Self Storage we know that our existence is possible because of you, our customer, and we want to make sure that you have the best customer experience possible when it comes to storing your belongings. In addition to storage we also provide packing supplies and U-Haul truck rentals. The Company prides itself on maintaining great facilities and employing the best managers in the industry. We look forward to satisfying your self-storage needs.

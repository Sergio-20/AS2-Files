About American Self Storage

American Self-Storage is an Oklahoma owned and managed company with 23 facilities to serve you, and to satisfy your self-storage needs. We know that our existence is possible because of you, our customer, and we want to make sure that you have the best customer experience possible when it comes to storing your belongings. We pride ourselves on maintaining great facilities and employing the best managers in the industry. We look forward to satisfying your self-storage needs!

Among our family of companies are American Self-Storage, Shawnee Self-Storage, Kickapoo Self-Storage, and 74th Street Self-Storage.

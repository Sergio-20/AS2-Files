Fun Facts about city, state:

Patience Latting was the first female mayor of Oklahoma City, OK, elected in 1971. She was also the first female mayor in the US to serve a city with a population of over 350,000.

In 2009, Interstate I-40 was approved to be moved one mile south as part of the “Core to Shore” MAP3 initiative of the city. This allowed the city to expand to the Oklahoma River shore while beautifying the entrance to the city.

Named one of the “Best places to live in North America” by Places Rated Almanac.

Oklahoma City is the birthplace of the shopping cart, invented and used in 1937 at Standard Food Markets.

Oklahoma City is equal distance from Los Angeles and New York.

Oklahoma City was the result of a land rush event known as “The Land Run” on April 22, 1889. Around 10,000 people settled Oklahoma City overnight, and population grew very fast. Now it is home to close to 615,000 citizen. Oklahoma City is the largest city in Oklahoma, covering several counties. There is no shortage of fun, culture, or entertainment, and Oklahoma has such a diverse population that it is perfect for anyone of any background or income bracket.
